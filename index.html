Strict Quality Control Ensure Your Sales Are Worry-Free

From the first steel plate to the final packaging, every Safewell tool cabinet undergoes a multi-stage, rigorous inspection process designed to exceed the highest industry standards.

IQC

Verifying the quality and specifications of all incoming steel and components.

IPQC

Ensuring dimensional accuracy and structural integrity during every manufacturing step.

Surface Coating Quality Control

Testing coating adhesion, thickness, and corrosion resistance for a flawless, durable finish.

Assembly Functional Control

Testing all moving parts like drawers, casters, and locks to guarantee smooth performance.

FQC

A complete visual and structural check of every finished unit before it leaves the line.

OQC

Ensuring secure packaging and accurate order fulfillment for safe transit.
